śrutismr̥tipurāṇānāmālayaṁ karuṇālayam ।
namāmi bhagavatpādaśaṅkaraṁ lōkaśaṅkaram ॥
prātaḥ pākaṁ vidadhatī pitāmahī rāghāvāṣṭakaṁ sasvaraṁ vadati sma । tadanusmaran idaṁ likhāmi । stōtramidaṁ śrīrāmōpari śaṅkarabhagavatpādēna racitaṁ vidyatē ।

rāghavaṁ karuṇākaraṁ munisēvitaṁ suravanditaṁ jānakīvadanāravindadivākaraṁ gunabhājanam ।
vālisūnuhitaiṣiṇaṁ hanumatpriyaṁ kamalēkṣaṇaṁ yātudhānabhayaṅkaraṁ praṇamāmi rāghavakuñjaram ॥
rāghavaṁ raghukulē bhavaṁ   karuṇākaraṁ dayāvantaṁ munisēvitaṁ r̥ṣiśritaṁ suravanditaṁ dēvapūjitaṁ jānakīvadanāravindaṁ jānakī janakatanayā sītā

raḥ sajau jabharā vadēdiha mattakōkilavr̥ttakam । asya mallikāmālēti kēcanābhidadhatē ।
